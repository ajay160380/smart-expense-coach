Our Story

Making finance effortless.

ExpenseTracker was built because we were tired of complex apps. We believe that tracking expenses should be as easy as sending a text message to a friend.

🚀

Our Mission

To eliminate the friction of personal finance. We noticed a pattern: people want to track their money, but the habit breaks because opening an app for a ₹50 coffee is tedious. By bringing the tracker to WhatsApp, we ensure your financial awareness never drops.

💡

The "Aha" Moment

Why build another app when 2 billion people already live on WhatsApp? That realization was the birth of ExpenseTracker.

🤖

AI Powered

We leverage cutting-edge Large Language Models to understand the context of your texts, so you don't have to manually select categories.

🛡️

Privacy First

Your financial data is highly sensitive. We built ExpenseTracker with military-grade encryption and a strict policy: we never sell your data, and our AI only processes your messages for expense categorization.

How We Got Here

Early 2026

The Frustration

After downloading the 5th budget tracker app and deleting it a week later, our founder realized the problem wasn't the apps, it was the friction of opening them.

Mid 2026

The Prototype

A basic script was built connecting WhatsApp to a spreadsheet. Friends loved it so much they begged for access. The LLM integration followed shortly after.

Today

ExpenseTracker Launch

ExpenseTracker officially launches, bringing frictionless AI-powered financial coaching to thousands of daily spenders.
