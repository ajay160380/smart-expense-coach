Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At PaisaMitra, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, and encrypted password.
  • WhatsApp Data: Your linked phone number and the expense messages you send to our bot.
  • Financial Data: The budgets you set and the expenses you record to provide you with insights.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our expense tracking services.
  • Analyze spending habits through our AI to offer personalized financial coaching.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account and service updates.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your financial records are strictly confidential and are only accessed by our automated AI systems to generate insights for your dashboard.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact Support page.
