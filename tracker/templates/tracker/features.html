Features

PaisaMitra is packed with intelligent features designed to make tracking expenses as frictionless as possible.

💬 WhatsApp Integration

Don't want to install another app? No problem. Simply message our WhatsApp bot with your expense, like "500 for lunch", and it instantly gets logged to your dashboard.

🧠 AI-Powered Categorization

Our intelligent AI analyzes your messages and automatically categorizes your spending. Whether it's groceries, petrol, or entertainment, the bot knows where it belongs.

📊 Real-time Dashboard

Visit your secure web dashboard to see a beautiful visual breakdown of your monthly spending, remaining budget, and category-wise analysis.

🤖 Brutally Honest Coach

Our AI acts as your personal financial coach. If you're spending too much, it won't hold back. It will analyze your habits and send you personalized, sarcastic, yet helpful advice to keep you on track.
